Validate Member

This takes a location ID and a barcode. The location ID is the ID of the gym the member is trying to sign into and the barcode is the member's barcode. This should block from any gym within 50 miles of a location of a gym they are a member of, unless they are a member of the gym they're logging into. This means if they are a member of 3 gyms, it will block all locations from all 3 organizations.
